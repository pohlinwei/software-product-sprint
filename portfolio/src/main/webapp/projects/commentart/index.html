Why these colours?

These colours are influenced by the sentiments of the comments and replies posted below. Are you interested in the mechanism of how this works? Click here to find out more!

Can I interact with it?

Of course! Click on Our Art above to filter the comments by date. Then, the resulting painting will be restricted to the sentiments of comments from that period of time. Alternatively, if you feel like contributing, leave a comment below!
